Become Energy Efficient with these 4 Smart Hacks

Introduction

Who couldn’t use a little extra cash?

In this economy, people are seen saving a buck or two wherever possible.All those small savings add up over time and come handy when faced with a nasty electric bill!ChoiceGenie suggests these 5 easy energy efficient life hacks for your family to save money on energy bills.

Smart Hack # 1: Unused Electronic UnPlugged: Check!

Did you know the devices consume electricity when they’re not being used?

An easy way to remedy this is to unplug electronic devices asap when not in use.Make it a habit of unplugging electric devices like phone chargers, small appliances, and lamps around the house when unused!It will turn into noticeable savings.

Smart Hack # 2: Turn the Computer Off, NOW!

Now that we are on the subject of turning off electronics, the biggest energy-vampire in every home is the computer.

Do you leave the desktop running non-stop just because it's a pain to wait for the machine to power up?

Well, you are in for a rude awakening when the bill comes forth and you end up paying several dollars a month.

Not worth it, really!

Smart Hack # 3: Turn that AC of yours OFF!

People that are used to set the temperature on the thermostat low and run the air conditioning all day during the summer.

And during the winter, cannot help but crank up the heat.

Well, as you can image, those people have to pay hefty bills!

While you can't control your utility rates, you can work on ways to lower your energy usage and keep that electric bill reasonable.

Just to lower the cost of cooling, how about starting to turn the air conditioner off every time you leave the house?

The hack is yours to borrow!

Smart Hack # 4: Turn the Unnecessary Lights Off!

What good is a light when no one is around to enjoy it?

Of course, keeping the lights on makes one feel safe, but letting the lights shine 24/7 uses loads of electricity and empties your pockets.That's why ChoiceGenie encourages to install energy efficient light bulbs. Those energy efficient light bulbs save electricity as well ass last longer than your traditional bulbs.

Bottom Line

Turn your home into a smart home!

It will let you track the energy consumption so you are devoid of surprises when the bill comes.
