Money Saving Hacks for your Electricity Bill

Introduction

Does not matter what season we’re in, the crazy electricity bill seems to be getting out of control every time.

Between the AC and all other necessary appliances and gadgets that our modern life calls for, we spend so much energy that the electricity bill is one of the biggest monthly expenses for most American households.

ChoiceGenie has come up with some easy hacks to reduce your energy consumption!

Whether you live in an apartment or a house, these hacks are guaranteed to cut your electricity bill in half.

Ready to save some hard earned dollars?

Hack # 1: Cleanse the Light Fixtures!

There is a high chance of better and brighter light when you regularly clean the light fixtures!

Moreover, as the light intensity of the bulbs gets cleaner, you can get away with turning on fewer lights

Hack # 2: Cleanse the Swimming Pool Pump

ChoiceGenie suggests you to frequently clear your strainer baskets in the swimming pool pump.

As clogged strainer basket can cause an increase in the energy consumption.

Hack # 3: Make Room for Cooling

Did you know that there is a mechanism drawing air into your fridge and freezer?

Yes, and which ends with a vent serving as an outlet for the drawn air.

Avoid putting the food right in front of that vent. You will be doing nothing but choking the fridge.

Hack # 4: Bring On the Task Lighting

It will be helpful to start using localized lighting when you work on the computer.

You need only a particular space to be lighted. When your electronic gadgets are probably already powered up, you don’t need to consume more energy than is necessary.

Turn your home into a smart home!

It will let you track the energy consumption so you are devoid of surprises when the bill comes.

Hack # 5: Consecutively Clean the Laundry

Do try to do a few loads of laundry at a time. Take advantage of the warm dryer.

Instead of having the dryer go from cold to warm, you should use it when it’s already warm. Your clothes will dry faster as the dryer does not have to run as long.

Hack # 6: Home Office: Unplugged!

If you are working from home, you most probably have a home office.

Although it does not mean your home office has to do everything that normal business office does. ChoiceGenie recommends to try not to plug every machine on at all times. When the printer has to be used once every 2 days, then avoid plugging it on 24/7.
