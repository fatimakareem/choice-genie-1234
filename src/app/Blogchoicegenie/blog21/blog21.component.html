Introduction

Saving money on energy is not only good for your pocket, but it also favors the environment!

With these awesome hacks, you can potentially save hundreds of dollars just by making a few easy changes. – ChoiceGenie.

STEP # 1: Turn Thermostat Down

Did you know that turning your thermostat down by 1°C can save you over $80 per year?

Well, we bet!

Keeping your heating on constantly and at low consumption will potentially save you a lot of money compared to switching on and off a few times per day.

If your house is well insulated, set your thermostat to 21°C (69 °F).

STEP # 2: Switch from Electric to Central Heating

Gas central heating is about 3-4 times cheaper than electricity per kWh.

So if your house is still using electric heating, you should consider switching to central heating.

Some Statistical Assurance: When you use an average of $80 per month on electric heating, that’s $960 per year!

So by changing to central heating, you will be able to save approximately a 75% of the money, which means saving $720/year.

STEP # 3: How about Batch Cooking for a Change?

ChoiceGenie encourages the Genie readers to consider cooking in bulk.

Thank you ChoiceGenie – on behalf of all the readers –, now you know by doing batch cooking, you’ll save a lot of electricity.

This will save you not only money on energy but what’s even more important, a lot of time!
