Easy Hacks to Make Energy Bill Lower & Pockets Heavier

Introduction

ChoiceGenie makes it unbelievably easy for you to reduce your energy consumption and lower your bills.

We will not talk about reducing your electric bill illegally with aluminum foil or magnets!

We’re going to share some easy hacks to make your pockets heavier & electric bill legally lower.

A Trick to Secure Pockets # 1: Run the Dishwasher Only When Full

Do not even think of turning on your dishwasher, until you have the full load

What will happen, you turn on your dishwasher when having less than a full load and you will do nothing but waste all that power and water in the long run.

A Trick to Secure Pockets # 2: Use One Charger

ChoiceGenie suggests to rotate cordless phones in one charger. According to our research, using just one charger at a time could yield decent saving.

A Trick to Secure Pockets # 3: Make Use of Standby Feature

Take advantage of the standby feature of your computer.

Manipulate this standard feature easily and wisely!

Set the time-out period and your computer powers down on your instruction.

A Trick to Secure Pockets # 4: Live a Day without Light!

Have a light free day, you will survive it!

Do without light for just one day every week.

Challenge your spouse, children or family to a unique lifestyle minus light for just one single day, heck for the fun of it!

No need to succumb to the total darkness, enjoy a candle-lit dinner instead.

A Trick to Secure Pockets # 5: Disconnect Microwave Light

A fun fact? Microwave light can be disconnected!

Why is the microwave oven need to be equipped with a light bulb, to begin with?

Why? When most people who can afford to invest in a microwave oven probably enjoy immaculate lighting at their kitchens.

But again we are on the serious subject of saving electricity, unplug the light bulb with the fact that it is not going to be a life-changing experience.

A Trick to Secure Pockets # 6: Unload your Manual Gardening Tools!

ChoiceGenie suggests using manual gardening tools.

You know you can just as effectively trim those bushes and hedges in your garden with manual shears as with powering up your electric trimmers.
